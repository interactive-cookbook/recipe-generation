Sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar in a large mixing bowl.
Measure milk in a jug.
Separate eggs.
Place egg whites in the bowl of a stand mixer.
Add the yolk and vanilla essence to milk.
Whisk together.
Pour in flour mixture.
Stir very gently until about combined.
Melt butter.
Stir in butter.
Continue mixing very gently until combined.
Beat egg whites until stiff.
Preheat waffle iron.
Slowly fold in egg whites into batter.
Save batter in batches on waffle iron.
Cook batter according to directions.
Remove immediately.
Serve with maple syrup and fruit.
