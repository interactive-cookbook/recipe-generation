In a large bowl beat sugar and vegetable oil to blend, using an electric mixer.
Lightly oil 2 9x6 loaf pans.
Add the eggs one at a time, then add the vanilla, mix after each addition.
Add bananas to combine.
Stir to combine.
In another large bowl, whisk together the fluid, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
Stir the flour mixture into the banana mixture 2 or 3 times until just combined.
Spoon evenly into loaf pans and fill them with batter about 3-4 ways to the top of each pan.
Fold in blueberries.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Preheat oven.
Insert toothpick in middle of loaf.
Bake in the oven for 60-70 minutes or until the toothpick comes out clean.
The loaf pan on a wire shelf, cooled for 10 minutes.
Remove pan from pan.
Place the Pan on a shelf.
Cool completely.
