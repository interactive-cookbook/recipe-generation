Lightly salt water.
Bring large pot of water to a boil.
Add ziti pasta.
Cook ziti pasta until al dente about 8 minutes.
Drain.
In a large skillet, fry onion and ground beef over medium heat.
Add spaghetti sauce and mushrooms.
Simmer 15 minutes.
Counter either a 9x13 baking dish.
Layer half the ziti in the baking dish.
In a small bowl, mix sour cream and cottage cheese together until blended.
Pour 1/2 of the meat sauce mixture over ziti.
Lay the provolone cheese slices over the meat sauce.
Spread the sour cream / cottage cheese mixture over the provolone cheese slices.
Spread the remaining ziti.
Top with the remaining meat sauce.
Shred mozzarella cheese.
Top with mozzarella cheese.
Preheat the oven to 25 degrees F.
Bake for 30 minutes or until cheeses are melted and the top starts to brown.
