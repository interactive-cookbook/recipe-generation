Using the paddle attachment, beat the butter, cream cheese, light brown sugar, granulated sugar and vanilla extract until the mixture becomes light and fluffy.
In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
Add eggs, one at a time, and make sure they are fully incorporated.
One at the time, add dry mixture until mixed.
Make sure the eggs are fully incorporated and scrape down the sides as necessary.
Stir in the chocolate chips.
Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Using a cookie scoop, place a tablespoon-2 mounds about 2 inches apart on the parchment paper.
Preheat the oven to 350.
Bake the cookies for 12-14 minutes or until brown.
Remove from oven.
Let cool for 5 minutes.
Transfer to cooling rack.
Transfer dough.
Place dough in refrigerator for overnight or at least 30 minutes for the best result.
