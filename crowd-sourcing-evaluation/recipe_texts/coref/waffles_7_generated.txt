In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
In a jug measure the milk.
Separate the eggs.
Place the eggs whites in the bowl of the stand mixer.
Add the yolk and vanilla essence to the milk.
Whisk together the yolk and vanilla essence.
Pour it into the flour mixture.
It stir it very gently until about combined.
Melt butter.
Stir butter into it.
Continue to mix butter very gently until combined.
Beat in to egg whites until stiff.
Preheat waffle iron.
Slowly fold in to batter the egg whites.
Sever the batter by batch onto the waffle iron.
Cook the egg whites according to your directions.
Remove the egg whites immediately.
Serve the maple syrup and fruit.
