Pour in to garlic water and chicken broth chicken.
Case garlic with cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper and bay leaf.
Stir in to garlic corn and cilantro.
Cover garlic.
Cook garlic on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
Brush both sides of pans with oil.
Cut tortoise into strips.
Place strips on a baking sheet.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees C.
Bake strips for about 10-15 minutes until crisp.
