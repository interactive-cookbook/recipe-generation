Pull the leaves off the cauliflower.
Cut the core out in kind of cone shape.
Break the wow or cut the wow regularly in about 1.5 inches.
Put the wow into the steamer insert.
Bring about 1 tbsp water to a boil in a steam pot, cover.
About 1 tbsp water add to about 1 inch of water inserted, cover.
About 1 tbsp water add to about 1 inch of water inserted cover and cook about 1 tbsp water for about 20 minutes (you may poke a paring knife through the stem or you may still feel a little texture in about 1 inch of water inserted ).
Dump about half of the cauliflower into the food processor.
Pour in to about half the cauliflower the nut milk, puree about half the cauliflower until smooth.
Now dump the rest of the cauliflower on top.
The rest of the cauliflower now puree the cauliflower again.
You need to stop, scrape and stir the puree a few times.
Add to the puree the butter, salt, pepper and garlic until smooth.
Smoothly mix about half the cauliflower.
Taste the cauliflower pure to season.
If you do n’t eat immediately, you can reheat the dear purée on the same pan over low heat.
Serve the purée hot.
