Preheat oil and loaf pan.
Add flour, oat, salt, cinnamon, pumpkin pie spice, baking powder, and flour, oats to large mixing bowl.
Stir it to combine.
In another mixing bowl, beat eggs with an electric hand mixer.
In another mixing bowl, then add sugar to eggs.
Beat eggs until well combined.
Add oils, yogurt, pumpkin and vanilla to eggs.
To eggs beat eggs to mix well.
Fake mini chips into batter.
Pour eggs into loaf pan.
Insert toothpick in middle.
Bake eggs 1 hour or until toothpick comes out clean.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees, and grease loaf pan.
