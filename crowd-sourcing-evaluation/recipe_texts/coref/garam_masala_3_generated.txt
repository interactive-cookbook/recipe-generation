In a small pan roast the coriander, cumin, seeds, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves separately.
Once the cloves start to smell fragrant, turn each one on a plate to cool.
Roast the cloves.
Strip the cards and use only the seeds.
Strip the cards and discard the seeds.
Put all the cards in an electric blender.
All the cards blend the cards into a fine powder.
Greased the nutmeg finely.
Mix the nutmeg finely.
Store the nutmeg in an airtight glass jar.
