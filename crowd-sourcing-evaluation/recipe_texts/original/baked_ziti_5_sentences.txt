Preheat oven to 350F.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add ziti pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain.
In a large skillet, brown onion and ground beef over medium heat.
Add spaghetti sauce and mushrooms, and simmer 15 minutes.
In a small bowl, mix the sour cream and cottage cheese together until blended.
Butter a 9Ã—13 inch baking or casserole dish.
Layer in the baking dish 1/2 of the cooked ziti.
Pour 1/2 of the meat sauce mixture over the ziti.
Lay the provolone cheese slices over the meat sauce.
Spread the sour cream / cottage cheese mixture over the provolone slices.
Spread the remaining ziti, and top with the remaining meat sauce mixture.
Top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
Bake for 30 minutes, or until cheeses are melted and top starts to brown.
