Add the flour mixture and mix on low speed just until blended .
Transfer the dough onto a piece of plastic wrap and wrap tightly .
In a medium bowl , whisk together both flours , the baking soda and salt . 
When ready to bake the cookies , preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. 
Refrigerate for at least 2 hours , or up to 5 days .
Bake for another 6 to 8 minutes , or until the cookies are dark golden brown .
Line two baking sheets with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat .
Store in an airtight container for up to 5 days .
Bake the cookies for 10 minutes and then rotate the baking sheet .
Let cool for a minute on the baking sheets , then transfer to a cooling rack .
Transfer to the baking sheet.
On a lightly floured surface , roll out the dough and cut into 3-inch squares .
Let cool completely ( they will crisp up as they cool ) .
