First four ingredient in a large mixing bowl combine well-combined.
Evenly spreading a 9x13 baking dish.
Combine ingredients for the peanut butter bar chocolate topping medium glass bowl.
Melt and in the microwave for 1 minute at a time.
From remove the microwave and stirred until smooth.
Spread of the peanut butter bar base.
Place in the freezer for 15 minutes.
Removes from the freezer.
Cut into individual bars.
The size depending on number of bars like you serve (20-36 bars).
