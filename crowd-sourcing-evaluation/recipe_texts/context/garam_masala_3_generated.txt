In a small pan roast separate coriander, cumin, seeds, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves.
Whenever each one starts to smell the fragrant spices turning onto a plate to cool.
Roast.
Strip out the cards and use only the seeds.
Strip the cards and discard the pods.
Put all in an electric blender.
Blend to a fine powder.
Grate finely the nutmeg.
Mix.
Store in an airtight glass jar with the lid.
