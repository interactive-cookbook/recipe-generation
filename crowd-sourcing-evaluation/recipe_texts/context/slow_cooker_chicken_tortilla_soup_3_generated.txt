Place chicken, tomatoes, enchilada sauce, onion, green chiles, and garlic into a slow cooker.
Pour in water and chicken broth.
Season with cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper, and bay leaf.
Stir in corn and cilantro.
Cover and simmer.
Cook on LOW for 6-8 hours or HIGH for 4-3 hours.
Brush both sides of pans with oil.
Cut pans into strips.
Place on baking sheet.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees C.
Bake until crisp, about 10 to 15 minutes.
To serve, sprinkle tortilla strips over top of soup.
