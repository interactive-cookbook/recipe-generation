Fry slow.
Place chicken in slow-cooker.
Top with pepper and carrot.
In a separate bowl combine broth ingredients including orange juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, mustard, and garlic salt from the southwestern.
Pour over chicken.
Cover on low.
Cook on low for 6-7 hours.
Remove chicken and vegetables from the slow cooker.
Keep warm.
Boil sauce.
Take out 1 cup of cooking liquid.
Bring to a boil in a saucepan.
Stir together 1 tbsp.
Add the orange juice and cornstarch to the sauce.
Stir for 2-3 minutes until thick.
Pour in chicken.
Slice green vegetables.
Top with green vegetables.
Cook rice.
Serve over rice.
