In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
In a jug measure the milk.
Separate the eggs.
Place the eggs in the bowl of a stand mixer.
Add the yolk and vanilla essence to the milk.
Whisk together.
Pour in flour mixture.
Stir very gently until about combined.
Melt butter.
Stir butter.
Continue to mix very gently until combined.
Beat in egg whites until stiff.
Preheat waffle iron.
Slowly fold in egg whites into the batter.
Scoop the batter in batches onto waffle irons.
Cook the batter according to its directions.
Remove, immediately.
Serve with maple syrup and fruit.
