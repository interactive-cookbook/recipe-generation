Preheat oil, you and the loaf pan.
In a large mixing bowl, add the flour, oats, salt, cinnamon, pumpkin pie spice, and baking powder.
Stir to combine.
In another mixing bowl, beat eggs with an electric hand mixer.
In another mixing bowl, then add sugar.
Beat until well combined.
Add the oil, yogurt, pumpkin, and vanilla.
Beat the oil, yogurt, pumpkin, and vanilla well to mix.
Fake the mini chips into the batter.
Pour into loaf pan.
Insert toothpick in the middle.
Bake for 1 hour or until toothpick comes out clean.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees, scoop out loaf pan.
