Grease and flour your loaf pan.
In a large mixing bowl, add flour, oats, salt, cinnamon, pumpkin pie spice and baking powder.
Stir to combine.
In another mixing bowl, beat eggs with an electric hand mixer.
Then add sugar in another mixing bowl.
Beat until well combined.
Add oil, yogurt, pumpkin and vanilla.
Beat in oil, yogurt, pumpkin, and vanilla to mix well.
Fake mini chips into batter.
Pour into loaf pan.
Insert a toothpick in the middle.
Bake for 1 hour or until toothpick comes out clean.
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees, and grease loaf pans.
