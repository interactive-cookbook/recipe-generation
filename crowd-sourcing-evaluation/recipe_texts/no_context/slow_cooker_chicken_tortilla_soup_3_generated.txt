Place chicken, tomatoes, onions, green chiles, and garlic in a slow cooker with enchilada sauce.
Pour water and chicken broth.
Vary cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper, and bay leaf.
Stir in corn and cilantro.
Cover and low.
Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 4-3 hours.
Brush both sides of the pan with oil.
Cut the pan into strips.
Place on baking sheet.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees C.
Bake until crisp, about 10-15 minutes.
To serve, mix tortilla strips on top of soup.
