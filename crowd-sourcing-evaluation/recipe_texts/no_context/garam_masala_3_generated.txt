In a small pan, separate the coriander, cumin, seeds, cardamom, cinnamon and nut.
When each one starts to smell the fragrant's turning onto a plate to cool.
Roast.
Strip the card and use only the seeds.
Strip the card and discard the pods.
Put all in an electric blender.
Blend to a fine powder.
Grease finely with nutmeg.
Mix.
Store in an airtight lid glass jar.
