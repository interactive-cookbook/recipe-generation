Salt water lightly.
Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
Add ziti pasta.
Cook until al dente (about 8 minutes ).
Drain.
In a large skillet fry onion and ground beef over medium heat.
Add spaghetti sauce and mushrooms.
Simmer 15 minutes.
Counter with a 9x13 baking dish or casserole dish.
Layer half of the ziti in a baking dish.
In a small bowl, mix together the sour cream and cottage cheese until blended.
Pour half the meat sauce mixture over the ziti.
Lay the provolone cheese slices over the meat sauce.
Spread sour cream/ cottage cheese mixture over provolone slices.
Spread remaining ziti.
Top with remaining meat sauce mixture.
Shred mozzarella cheese.
Top with mozzarella cheese.
Preheat oven to 25 degrees F.
Bake for 30 minutes or until cheeses are melted and the top starts to brown.
