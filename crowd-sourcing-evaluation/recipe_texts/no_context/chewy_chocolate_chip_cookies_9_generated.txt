Using a paddle attachment, beat butter, cream cheese, light brown sugar, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until mixture becomes light and fluffy.
In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
Add eggs one at a time, making sure to incorporate fully.
Add dry mixture one at a time until mixed.
Make sure the eggs are fully incorporated, scrape down the sides as necessary.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Using a cookie scoop, place a tablespoon to 1 1/2 inches apart on parchment paper.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Bake the cookies for 12-14 minutes or until golden.
Remove from oven.
Let cool for 5 minutes.
Transfer to a cooling rack.
Knead dough.
Place the dough in the refrigerator for the best result for overnight or at least 30 minutes.
