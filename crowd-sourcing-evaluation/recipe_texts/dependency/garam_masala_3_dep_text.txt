In a small pan roast separately the coriander, cumin, peppercorns, cardamom, cinnamon and cloves.
As each one starts to smell fragrant.
Turn on to plate cool.
To.
After roast, peel.
And use only the seeds.
Discard pods.
Put all into electric blender.
And blend to a fine powder.
Finely grate nutmeg.
And mix in.
Store in glass jar with airtight lid.
